FIGMA

Omada Wine and Deli sought a conceptual design for their newly opened deli store in Copenhagen. It was essential that the visual identity harmonized with their existing color scheme, characterized by their distinctive red in combination with white. Given that the website's primary purpose wasn't online sales but rather to highlight the essence of the store and its numerous events, we developed a one-page design. This design placed focus on their unique "plat du jour" concept, wine tastings, and exclusive products.

In the project, my main responsibilities was centered on the coding aspects of the website and its functionalities. This included the development of features like the Plat du Jour suggestion section and the product slider. The primary focus for the page was on responsive web design, allowing for seamless viewing across all platforms. Furthermore, we produced promotional marketing videos, one of which is displayed in the top hero section of the page.

Adobe XD

"Coffee First" was my initial client project, involving a complete redesign of the website for a dog-friendly coffee café. The website consists of three pages, with the primary page being the homepage, where customers can conveniently access essential information such as opening hours, location details, and the menu, all thoughtfully combined onto a single page. We wished to create an elegant design, using their main beige color scheme with a refreshing splash of green. What makes this project especially significant is that the client opted to independently implement the design onto their existing website.

My primary role centered on the coding and implementation of the website, given that I was the lead website developer for the project. The site was constructed using a grid structure primarily featuring two columns, mirroring a menu card layout. To encapsulate the essence of the café, we crafted a promotional video, which is found on the "About Us" page.
